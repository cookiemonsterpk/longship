Case Study

Momentous

Momentous was planning on integrating additional features into Zip.ca and needed to find a way to incorporate the new features without over complicating the site for its users.

Working with the development team, Longship evaluated the existing site and then architected a new interface that streamlined the interface while adding the additional features.

Longship is extremely knowledgeable about user experience design and strategy. They listened closely to our needs, and offered solutions that met both business objectives and complex requirements, on time and on budget. Their smart insight helped us turn intricate scenarios into simple user-friendly experiences. I look forward to the opportunity to work with them again on future web projects.
Jana Dybinski, Vice President Marketing
