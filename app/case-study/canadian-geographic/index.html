Case Study

Canadian Geographic

Working with Canadian Geographic since 2006, Longship has provided a wide range of services ranging from strategy development, to content research and web development. Longship has worked onsite with Canadian Geographic for over a year providing in-house expertise on digital strategy and communications. We are a fully embedded member of the CG team.

Canadian Geographic has worked with Erik Hagborg on numerous projects over the years. The projects have ranged from interactive touchscreen games to complicated and detailed mapping projects. Erik has always understood our projects better than just about any other contractor I have ever worked with. He has great ideas and vision, plans projects exceptionally well and always works collaboratively and collegially.

I would recommend Longship for any assignment that requires vision, direction and strong leadership.
Gilles Gagnier, Vice President Content Creation
