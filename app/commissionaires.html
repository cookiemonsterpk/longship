Case Study

Commissionaires

The Commissionaires, Canada's premiere security firm, hired Longship in 2012 to provide consultation and advice with regards to its web presence. Working with the Commissionaires over the course of the past two years, Longship has helped implement a "User Centric" attitude about the web.

Recently Longship completed the planning for the new Commissionaires web site which has provided a number of solutions to their digital communications challenges. A key part of this approach is the implementation of an effective measurement system to enable proper tracking of their web marketing efforts and the ability to adjust and improve overtime.
